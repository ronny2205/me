About Me

I am a full-stack developer, just as excited to dive into the back end or the front end, to write algorithms or to design great user experience, or to exercise my logical or creative skills. My love for programming means that I am constantly pushing myself to code something new, level-up my skills, and become a better developer.

I cut my teeth in software development in the Israeli startup scene, after I completed an exclusive and intensive computer science training course in the Israeli army. I am now excited to have returned to coding after working for several years as a playwright and math teacher.

Recently, I gave my skills a boost by completing the Firehose coding bootcamp for web development. Since then, I have been immersed in several of my own projects, working primarily in Ruby on Rails and React.js.

I'm ready to continue this adventure by joining a team where we can collaborate, take on challenges, build great products and have fun along the way.
